How to Help Your Student Succeed in Physics
 
Your involvement is critical to your student's success. Here are three ways that you can help your student succeed.
 
 
Check for Missing Assignments
 
With a Canvas Observer account, you can click "Grades" to see what assignments are missing or late. Remind your student to finish and submit assignments IF NECESSARY.
 
Encourage Improvement
 
ALL assignments can be resubmitted to improve scores. Use Canvas to check for low scores on any assignments. Communication with teacher is required for submitting late work.
 
Have Conversations
 
Ask your student about what they are learning in Physics. Discussing scientific ideas at home can help students develop scientific literacy and will improve their engagement in the class.
 
Stay Connected
 
Create a Canvas Observer Account to view your student's assignments and grades.
